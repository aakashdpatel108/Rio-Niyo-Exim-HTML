Exporting high-quality fruits, vegetables, and spices

At RioNiyo Exim, we take pride in bringing the richness of India’s agriculture to the world. Headquartered in India, we specialize in the export of premium-quality fruits, vegetables, and other agricultural products that meet global standards of freshness, nutrition, and sustainability.

With a strong network of farmers and suppliers, we ensure that every product we deliver is cultivated with care, harvested at its peak, and packed under strict quality controls. Whether you are a wholesaler, retailer, or direct consumer, RioNiyo Export is committed to providing reliable supply, competitive pricing, and unmatched service.

Our focus on organic practices, sustainable sourcing, and international certifications helps us meet the diverse needs of global markets while promoting India’s agricultural excellence worldwide.
